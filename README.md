FPL Copilot is a Fantasy Premier League decision engine that turns player projections, fixture difficulty, ownership, budget, and transfer rules into a clear weekly planning dashboard.

**Live app:** [fpl-copilot.tech](https://fpl-copilot.tech)

## Hook

Most FPL tools show stats. FPL Copilot answers the weekly decision: who should I captain, transfer, bench, or target before the deadline?

In one dashboard, the app combines machine-learning player projections, fixture-aware recommendations, low-ownership differential picks, saved-squad analysis, and a transfer-aware optimizer that accounts for budget, squad rules, free transfers, and points hits.

## Why It Matters

Fantasy Premier League is a strong real-world analytics problem because every recommendation has constraints: price, position, team limits, fixture difficulty, player availability, ownership, captaincy upside, and transfer cost.

FPL Copilot turns that messy decision space into a product workflow:

- predicts player points for the active gameweek
- recommends captaincy, differential, and fixture-based targets
- lets users save and evaluate their actual squad
- checks FPL squad and lineup rules automatically
- builds optimized transfer plans while accounting for points hits
- keeps the app usable even when live data is unavailable by falling back to saved model outputs
